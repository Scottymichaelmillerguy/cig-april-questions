What do Muslims celebrate as a holy month which began March 22nd of this year and runs through April 20th?

Answer: Ramadan

Ramadan also spelled Ramazan, Ramzan, Ramadhan or Ramathan is the ninth month of the Islamic Calendar, observed by Muslims world wide as a month of fasting(sawm), prayer, reflection and community. For 2023 it takes places between March 22nd and April 20th. The word Ramadan derives from the Arabic root R-M-Ḍ (ر-م-ض) "scorching heat"

What is Zakat?

One of the five acts of worship that are considered duties, Zakat is alms-giving, which is giving money or food to poor people. Usually a fixed portion 2.5% annually.

What is Dhu al-Hijjah?

Dhu al-Hijja is the twelfth and final month of the islamic calendar.

What is Hajj?

Another of the five acts of worship that are considered duties, Hajj is a pilgrimage to Mecca, Saudi Arabia.
